(Ratings 4.5/5)


(Starting only @600).....


Location-Main GT Karnal Road near GTB, Memorial, Delhi
Timings - 10 AM - 7 PM

About Water Park -

Delhi has quite a lot to offer when it comes to Adventure and Water Parks throughout the city, of which Just Chill Waterpark is one of the most notable ones. Just Chill Waterpark is great for visitors who are tired of the searing heat and want to take a break to cool off. Aside from being a waterpark, it is also an amusement park, an adventure park, and an educational park. So visit this amazing place with your family and friends to enjoy your heart out.


About the Facilities -

  • Arrive a the activity location and get ready to have a blast at the Just Chill Waterpark.

  • Enter into the park and jump into one of the many pools to cool off yourself and relax.

  • There are 12 amazing attractions with thrilling rides to enhace your experience including Rainbow, Katrina-Twist, Black Thrill, GodZilla and etc.

  • Numerous of exciting rides and activities to select from, including Caterpillar, Bull Ride, Columbus and in addition to wonderful water rides.

  • This park provides a wealth of educational opportunities as well. As well as to awaken your inner potter and learn various ceramic methods.

  • You can also book rooms here to stay and relax and take a break from all the hustle -bustle of your routine life.
