About Water Park -

The Fun N Food Village offers India's largest collection of Water slides and attractions for the toddler learning how to swim to the Water sports enthusiast seeking a thrill. Thrills' chills and spills don't miss them at the Fun n Food Village offers its patrons a new twisting turning and sliding experience with the longest Water slide in the Country.

Don’t miss the chance of enjoying a fun day outing session at the Fun n Food Village- Gather great entertainment for the entire family with a variety of water slides, games, and rides that are available for people of all age groups. The park is divided into two sections, Water Park and Amusement Park. The total number of rides in both parks is over 40. You have a wide variety of adventure rides and water slides. Let your junior buddies rejoice in their space with a separate water play zone for kids and glide through the water with them at the longest water slide in India.

They are also Including New Rides For more Fun, You can check Those updates By simply Clicking on the Below Book Now at the page end and also Books your Tickets.


About the Facilities -

  • Enjoy a day outing of 9 hours and spend a much-needed fun time with your loved ones.

  • Munch some amazing food and rejuvenate yourself at the same time as the amusement park boasts of the multi-cuisine food court, health club, and shopping center within the park premises.

  • Enjoy to the fullest by gushing through the longest water slide in India with your dearest friends or family.

  • The amusement park has 21 rides Some of the best-known rides here are Rock n Roll, Polo Trooper, Tora Tora, Magic Carpet, Water Merry, Eagle Ridge, and many more.

  • The water park has 22 water slides. The rain dance is a special attraction during the summer for both kids and adults.

  • The park also boasts of a separate water play zone for kids. It has the longest water slide in India and the fastest water slide in Asia.

  • Enjoy a complete day of fun and enjoyment and end this experience on a happy note.
