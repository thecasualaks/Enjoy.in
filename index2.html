About Water Park -

The best water park in Delhi NCR, Atlantic Water World is surround by a water canal and sits on the Yamuna waterfront along the Kalindi Kunj barrage. Atlantic Water World is a world class water park in Delhi/NCR, offering people the perfect getaway full of fun, thrill, sheer excitement and real entertainment. With 13 world class slides, some of which are exclusively available only at Atlantic Water World, like Aqua Funnel, Tornado - both of them, the first in North India.

Atlantic Water World provides a place where parents and children can share in the spirit of imagination and bring out their hidden child and adventure enthusiast through the whirling rides, water slides, entertaining shows, indoor games and activities, I will Personnel Recommed You to Visit there Atleast One Time.

About the Facilities -

  • Arrive at the activity location right on time to make the most out of your day at the water park.

  • The park has some of the notable water slides and amusement park rides ranging from the wave pool falls to roller-coasters.

  • Along with a Live DJ playing a tune to swing you to a thrilling mood. Chill out in the wave pool with chilling showers, enjoy the Kempty falls.

  • Hop on the biggest roller coaster in Delhi-NCR that will surely raise your excitement levels.

  • You can also enjoy various rides at the amusement park such as Rock N Roll, Disc Coaster, Revolving Tower, and much more.

  • The park also has a kids zone with Kids Rides like Mini Columbus, Toy Train, Ball Pool, etc & Mini Zoo.

  • You can also take part in enthralling activities such as Zipline, Wall Climbing, Rappeling, Commando Net, Burma Bridge, etc.

  • All activities will be accompanied by a highly trained staff & the park follows international standards for safety.

  • There are many eateries in Poolside food court, including quick bites, to make sure that you get the taste you are looking for all day long.


    • How to Reach?

    This park is located in the heart of Delhi and is well connected with the city, you can reach here easily by road. The park also has a parking location available. The nearest metro station would be Kalindi Kunj.
