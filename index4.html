About Water Park -

Spend a day out at one of the famous amusement and water parks in Delhi- The Adventure Island. This amusement park is spread over 62 acres of land divided into the amusement park and Lagoon - the artificial lake. The lake offers a unique boating experience for visitors. Have fun with an alluring rain dance and try the different water slides available inside the park. This park is known to be built in the highest and globally accepted European Safety Standards, making it a suitable day-out experience for all age groups.

You Will Never See these kind of Water & Amusement Park in Delhi, They have their own real Airplane Restaurant OMG! and a Beautiful lake Including a Eiffel Tower Bridge and having a Lot's of many more Activities to do there


About the Facilities -

  • Enjoy a day outing full of thrills and adventure, a day that you are surely never going to forget.

  • Get ready for supreme fun as you get the entry ticket to Adventure Island Park, the most engaging amusement park in Delhi.

  • Try out the most famous rides of the location that imply z-force, sidewinder, sky riders, lightning bolt, space jump & splashdown.

  • Enjoy the water slides such as an aqua bump, Amazon mist forest, Sha La La, splash dunk, splash down & H2O will leave you amazed.
